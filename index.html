Veggie Shinobi

Try to cut as many fruits as possible with your hands within 120 seconds. You have 3 lives.

If you cut the bomb, you lose one of the lives.

There is a chance that a special type of fruit will occasionally spawn.

If a blue fruit spawns on your screen and you cut it, time will slow down by half.

If you spawn a fruit with a pink color, you will get a score multiplier and your points will be doubled for each cut fruit.

Good luck.
